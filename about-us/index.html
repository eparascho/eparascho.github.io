About Us

FEW WORDS ABOUT US

We are a group of researchers as part of RAIS consortium aspiring to conduct a series of research experiments related to health and wellbeing using wearable and mobile sensing devices.

Real Time Internet of Sports (RAIS)

RAIS is a Marie Curie European Training Network covering a broad spectrum of subjects establishing a fertile inter-disciplinary research and innovation community that will advance:

watch

Wearable Technology

Wearable Sports Sensing and Quantified-self Devices and Accompanying Middleware

memory

Block-chain Powered IoT

Decentralized Block-chain Powered IoT Platforms (generating hundreds of billions of transactions per day) for Big Data Mining

trending_up

Real-time Edge Analytics

Real-time Edge Analytics And Predictive Modelling To Capture A Broad Range Of Sports-related Data And Trends (e.g., activities and contextual information), Critical In A Variety Of Application Settings
