Participant Instructions

RAIS USER EXPERIMENT

You are invited to take part in a research study aiming to recognize activities, and explore habits and behaviours in daily life from smartphone and wearable data. To join our study, please follow the steps below:

Instructions before we start collecting your data

  • The consent form is necessary to ensure that you are informed, comfortable and feel safe sharing your experiences, and

  • Allows you to have full control over your data

Fitbit Sense is an advanced smartwatch that will allow us to collect the needed data for our research. The data will be automatically collected daily and consist measurements from your daily life such as activities, heart rates, stress and sleep. In addition, at the end of the experiment we will ask you to provide us with the complete archive of your Fitbit account data

  • Once you receive your Fitbit Sense device, download and install the Fitbit app on your Android or iOS and create a Fitbit account

  • How do I get started with Fitbit Sense?

  • You can also find a user-manual provided by Fitbit here

  • How do I export my Fitbit account data?

  • If you face any technical difficulties on setting up Fitbit Sense, creating a Fitbit account, connecting the Fitbit Sense with your smartphone or any other related issue please reach us via our email: rais.experiment@gmail.com

AWARE Light is an application for data collection from smartphones using Experience Sampling. Experience Sampling is a method to measure your behaviour, thoughts, and feelings throughout daily life. Data is collected through self-reports completed at certain times during the day

  • The app is available for download here (Android only). You are advised to download the app from your smartphone device

  • Once you download and install the app you will then need to open the AWARE Light app, and paste the following link into it: https://drive.google.com/file/d/16WJ0t4lOk2PjSbq5XhcEPC_HmSAL6ran/view?usp=sharing

  • You are requested to keep the app running on your background

  • You are good to go!!!

This step is not mandatory however is important for us to collect additional data that will help us to analyze more efficiently your daily behavior

Instructions during your participation

  • Carry on with your daily life as you would normally do

  • You are requested to wear your Fitbit Sense and carry your smartphone as much as possible

  • To know you better we would like to respond to our daily questions that you will receive on your smartphone through the AWARE Light app. Furthermore, occasionally you will receive questionnaires on your email that help us understand different aspects of your personality and behavior.

    • Each question is expected to take 2-5 sec and each questionnaire 7-10 min

    • We kindly ask you to be persistent and accurate on your answers

  • For any question or difficulty you may face, feel free to contact us via our email: rais.experiment@gmail.com


Your participation is highly appreciated and we hope you will enjoy your experience with Fitbit
